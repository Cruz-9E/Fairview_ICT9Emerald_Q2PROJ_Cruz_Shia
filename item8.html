Pork Sinigang

Sinigang is a popular Filipino soup or stew that's known for its soury and savory flavor.


Ingredients:

  • - 2 lbs. pork belly
  • - 1 lb. young tamarind
  • - 1 bunch water spinach chopped
  • - 8 pieces string beans cut into 2-inch pieces
  • - 2 pieces eggplants sliced
  • - 1 piece daikon radish
  • - 8 pieces okras
  • - 2 pieces tomatoes sliced into wedges
  • - 2 pieces long green pepper
  • - 1 piece onion sliced into wedges
  • - 2 quarts water
  • - Fish sauce and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. 1. Boil the young tamarind in 2 quarts of water for 40 minutes. Filter the tamarind broth using a kitchen sieve or a strainer.
  2. 2. Pour the tamarind broth into a cooking pot. Let it boil and then add the onion, pork belly, and half the amount of the tomatoes.
  3. 3. Skim-off the floating scums, pour 1 tablespoon fish sauce, cover and continue to simmer for 1 hour.
  4. 4. Add daikon radish and eggplants. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. 5. Add the long green pepper, string beans, remaining tomatoes, and okra. Cook for 3 minutes.
  6. 6. Add the chopped water spinach stalks and season with fish sauce and ground black pepper. Cook for 2 minutes.
  7. 7. Put the water spinach leaves. Cover and turn the heat off. Let the residual heat cook the leaves for 3 minutes before serving.
  8. 8. Serve and enjoy!
