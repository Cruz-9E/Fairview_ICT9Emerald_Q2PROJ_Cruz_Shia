About Cruz Cuisine

Welcome to Cruz Cuisine, a place for you to discover the most popular and cherished recipes of the Philippines. Our webpage features a collection of delicious dishes that have been passed throughout generations in our nation.


At Cruz Cuisine, we believe that food isn't just for our nourishment. It's to celebrate culture, memories, and the joy of sharing a meal with our loved ones. Cruz Cuisine shares these recipes so that it is accessible to everyone. Recreate the magic of these Filipino dishes in your own kitchen, and enjoy.

"Kainan na!"

What We Offer:

1. A wide range of Filipino recipes from snacks, dishes, to desserts.

2. Step-by-step instructions in order to guide you through every dish.

3. The list of ingredients required to recreate every dish.

4. An opportunity to share and submit your recipes with the world.


Whether you're an experienced chef or a beginner, Cruz Cuisine is here to inspire you to get cooking and create these Filipino meals that everyone will love.
