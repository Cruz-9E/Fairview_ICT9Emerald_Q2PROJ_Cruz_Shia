Champorado Recipe

Champorado is a sweet chocolate rice porridge that uses sweet glutinous rice and cocoa powder as main ingredients.


Ingredients:

  • - tablea (5 pieces) or cocoa powder
  • - glutinous rice (1 3/4 cups)
  • - granulated white sugar (3/4 cups)
  • - water (6-8 cups)
  • - condensed milk

Instructions:

  1. 1. Boil the water.
  2. 2. Put in the tablea/cocoa powder and then stir. Let it dissolve in the water.
  3. 3. Add in the rice. Stir almost constantly to avoid sticking, and let the rice absorb the water.
  4. 4. Add the sugar. Stir until it dissolves.
  5. 5. Transfer the champorado in serving bowls. Top with condensed milk.
