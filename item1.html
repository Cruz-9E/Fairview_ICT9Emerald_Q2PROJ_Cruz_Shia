Banana Cue

Banana cue is a popular Filipino street food snack of deep-fried bananas coated in caramelized brown sugar and served on skewers.


Ingredients:

  • - 6 pieces saging na saba
  • - 2 cups brown sugar
  • - 4 cups cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. 1. Heat a cooking pot then pour-in cooking oil.
  2. 2. When the oil becomes hot, deep fry the bananas for 2 minutes.
  3. 3. Gradually put-in the brown sugar, adjust the heat to medium-low and continue cooking until the melted brown sugar coats the bananas.
  4. 4. Remove the cooked bananas one by one and immediately skewer using a bamboo skewer.
  5. 5. Share and enjoy.
